Planning an event?

We have two areas that can be closed off for private parties. One room seats up to eight people comfortably; it includes a television that can be used to project digital presentations, making it an ideal setting for working business lunch. Our larger area can accommodate up to forty people; The area can be completely closed off, partially enclosed, or left open so your larger party can mingle into the pub area.
